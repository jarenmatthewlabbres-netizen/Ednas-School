About EDNAS

School History:
  • 1973- The school offered a complete two-level instruction, pre-school and grade school.
  • 1977- Ednas School graduated its first batch of high school
  • 1984- Ednas School of San Carlos was opened
  • 1991- It integrated computer education in the curriculum of all grade levels including pre-school.
  • 1997- Ednas School turned 31 years old
  • 2000 - Pearl Anniversary, a three story building was built which houses the Accounting office,
    • MISSION
  • A god-centered Filipino school with a global outlook committed to the goals of nation-building and dedicated to the attainment of excellence in all facets of human life that lead to the advancement of knowledge and life long learning skills enhanced with generosity. faith, and life integration a school that gives light 10 the north and spreads the flame of prayer, ambition, and excellence to the country devoted to a standard quality of service for all learners of the country and members of community of nations
    • VISION
  • Ednas school is committed to the total formation of the entire man by the progressive and harmonious development of the moral, spiritual, intellectual, social aesthetic and physical faculties
    • CORE VALUES
  • A christian that:
      Prays daily, respects others beliefs,exercises christian values
  • A citizen who:
      Follows rules, greets elders, enjoys his/her rights as a filipino
  • A Filipino who:
      Signs the anthem, recites "Panatang Makabayan", practices hospitality
  • An Individual who:
      Comes to school on time, submits projects on time, greets teachers, speaks filipino and english
